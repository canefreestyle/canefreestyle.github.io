Cane Freestyle Contact

co-cane

I am filming footage for a cane freestyle video called "co-cane" (think Co-(llaboration) Cane). Cane freestyle is "the freedom & ability to move canes with diverse styles & techniques". It includes twirling, juggling, swinging, balancing, and combo techniques as well as creative and artistic concepts with one or more canes.

To participate in co-cane performers do not need to have great skill with canes; co-cane is not a skill competition. Typically, I select a talented performer and then choreograph, critique, coach, and capture a great video clip. While most clips don't make the cut, almost all of the hand-picked performers persevere! co-cane is centered on cameos, creativity, complexity, comedy, class, cooperation and cultural diversity.

As a result, co-cane is the Colossal Cinematic Cane Circus Collaboration!

So far, co-cane has:

  • 330 unique performers. Some performers contribute several clips.
  • 304 video clips. The average length of each clip is about 20 seconds.
  • 105 minutes of good footage (with no drops or big mistakes).
  • dozens of well-known professional performers and living legends.
The video has clip quality standards:
  • All clips show very unique performances.
  • All clips show the name of the performer.
  • All clips are filmed by the same person.
  • All clips use canes with the same design.
  • All clips are filmed with digital cameras.
  • All clips are "good takes" with no drops.
  • All clips are shot exclusively for co-cane.
  • All clips are numbered to ease discussion.
